### Featured Projects

**MedScribe** — clinical handwriting digitization
Turns photos of handwritten prescriptions into structured medication records with transformer OCR, routing low-confidence extractions to a clinician review queue.
`Python` `FastAPI` `PyTorch` `TrOCR` `OpenCV` `SQLAlchemy` `React`
-> Human-in-the-loop by design: every extracted field carries a confidence score and is traceable to evidence — nothing is silently accepted.

**ECG Arrhythmia Detection** — interpretable 5-class screening on MIT-BIH
Pan-Tompkins QRS detection, clinical feature extraction, and a hybrid rules + Random Forest classifier, served through a Flask API and React frontend.
`Python` `scikit-learn` `SciPy` `Flask` `React` `pytest` `GitHub Actions`
-> Validation as a first-class feature: record-grouped cross-validation to prevent patient leakage, bootstrap confidence intervals, and honestly reported metrics (ML sub-model ROC-AUC 0.915).

**Silent Swap** — adversarial clinical NLP study
A reproducible 540-run study injecting SNOMED sibling-drug swaps into clinical text to measure where a five-stage NLP pipeline first detects the corruption.
`Python` `PyTorch` `Bio_ClinicalBERT` `TrOCR` `SNOMED CT` `SciPy`
-> Publishes its negative result honestly — the swaps weren't stealthier than noise — and documents the real finding: a ~2x final-stage NER F1 collapse.

**Sahi** — AI nutrition decision app
React Native app for people managing chronic conditions: search, scan, or photograph a food and get an instant ally / caution / avoid verdict personalized to your conditions.
`React Native` `Expo` `TypeScript` `Supabase` `PostgreSQL` `Deno Edge Functions` `Gemini`
-> Production-minded AI: row-level security, per-user rate limiting, verdict caching, and safe-default fallbacks for every AI failure mode.

**SkillBridge** — career-path intelligence platform
~14k-LOC MERN platform matching users to careers via skills, certifications, and Big Five/MBTI scoring, with a gap-analysis engine and milestone roadmaps.
`React` `Redux Toolkit` `Node.js` `Express` `MongoDB` `JWT`
-> Feature-module architecture (controller/service/validators per domain) that keeps a 134-file codebase maintainable, with a self-check harness validating its own scoring logic.

**Synthetic Health Data** — generation and privacy audit
VAE and CTGAN (WGAN-GP) implemented from scratch in PyTorch to synthesize mental-health survey data, audited for statistical fidelity, downstream utility (TSTR), and privacy.
`Python` `PyTorch` `VAE` `CTGAN` `scikit-learn` `pandas`
-> Privacy claims backed by actual attacks — DOMIAS membership inference and calibrated distance-to-closest-record — not just distance metrics.
